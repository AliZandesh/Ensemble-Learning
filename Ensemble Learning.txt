

An Ensemble is a group of musicians, actors, or dancers who perform together, this gives us a better experience.
similarly, people started using a group of machine learning models to produce better prediction results.

we have categorized this ensemble strategy into 4 groups. 
Bagging, Boosting, stacking, and Cascading.
out of these 4, Bagging and Boosting are the most practical and widely used on various regression and classification problems.
